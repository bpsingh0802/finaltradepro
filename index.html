Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TradeConnect

Simply open the app and browse through service categories on the home screen. You can search by category, view featured professionals, or use filters like location and rating to find the perfect match for your job. Once you find someone, you can view their profile, portfolio, and book their services directly.

TradeConnect offers secure in-app payments. After a service is completed, you can pay directly through the app using your saved payment method. We hold the payment until you confirm the job is complete to your satisfaction. This provides protection for both customers and professionals. Professionals receive their payments within 1-2 business days.

To join as a professional, download the app and select "Professional" during registration. You'll need to complete your profile with your services, pricing, portfolio, and qualifications. We verify all professionals to ensure quality service. You'll also need to provide identification and any relevant trade licenses or certifications. Once approved, you can start receiving job requests.

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not satisfied with a service, please contact the professional directly through the app chat to resolve the issue. If you can't reach a resolution, you can file a complaint through our Help Center. Our support team will review the case and may offer mediation or a refund depending on the circumstances.

After finding a professional, click "Book Now" on their profile. Select the services you need, choose any additional options, and pick an available date and time. Review your booking details, add your address if needed, and confirm. The professional will receive your request and can accept or decline. Once accepted, you'll get a confirmation notification and can communicate directly with the professional.

For customers, there are no fees to use the app and book services. Professionals pay a 10% service fee on completed jobs, which covers payment processing, the verification process, marketing, and ongoing platform development. Premium subscription options are also available for professionals who want additional features like priority placement in search results.
